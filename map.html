About this map
Anthropogenic changes to the Earth's biosphere are now so advanced that they threaten the favorable conditions it provides for our species. Humanity faces great hardship in the middle to latter part of this century if we do not take the steps necessary to back away from planetary boundaries and to retain and restore the life-supporting function of our habitat. Here we propose a set of minimal actions that need to be taken in order to maintain a biosphere that will enable our species to persist into the future. The essence of these actions is to increase the level of protection for wild life and wild places several orders of magnitude and to stop poisoning our ecosystems. The result will be a balance of human and natural ecosystems, resilient in the face of disturbance and change and providing productive resources and sanctuaries for life.
